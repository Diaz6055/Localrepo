What's included in our 22-point AC tune-up

Refrigerant & cooling performance

  • Check refrigerant pressure (suction & liquid line)
  • Measure superheat and subcooling against manufacturer spec
  • Inspect for visible refrigerant leaks at fittings and coils
  • Verify proper temperature differential at the supply & return
  • Test thermostat calibration and operation

Electrical & mechanical

  • Test capacitor capacitance vs. rated MFD
  • Inspect & tighten all electrical connections
  • Measure compressor & fan motor amp draw
  • Inspect contactor for pitting or burned contacts
  • Lubricate motor bearings where applicable
  • Inspect blower wheel & belt

Coils, drains & airflow

  • Rinse outdoor condenser coil
  • Inspect indoor evaporator coil for dirt & ice formation
  • Clear condensate drain line, treat for algae
  • Inspect & replace standard 1-inch filter (if needed)
  • Check duct connections at the air handler for major leaks

Safety & final report

  • Inspect disconnect, fuses, and breaker
  • Verify safety controls and float switch
  • Document all measurements in a written report
  • Photograph any issues that may need attention

Common AC problems we fix every day

  • Warm air blowing from vents — usually a refrigerant leak, frozen coil, or bad compressor.
  • AC won't turn on — often a tripped breaker, blown capacitor, or faulty thermostat.
  • Loud or unusual noises — buzzing, rattling, screeching, or grinding all signal different mechanical issues.
  • Water leaking from indoor unit — typically a clogged condensate drain line or broken pump.
  • System short-cycling — turning on and off rapidly, draining your APS bill and shortening unit life.
  • Frozen evaporator coil — restricted airflow or low refrigerant; needs immediate attention.
  • High electricity bills — failing components force the system to work harder for less cooling.
  • Weak airflow — could be a dirty filter, blower motor failure, or duct issue.

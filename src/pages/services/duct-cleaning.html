What's hiding in your @@city ductwork right now?

If your home is more than 10 years old and you've never had your ducts inspected, the answer is usually unsettling. The desert dust that gets through your filter slowly accumulates inside the duct walls. Construction debris from the original build is often still in there. Pet dander, pollen, dead bugs, and in some cases mold — all of it gets recirculated through the air your family breathes every day.

That's the cleaning side of the equation. The sealing side is just as important: studies from the Department of Energy show that the average home loses 20-30% of conditioned air through duct leaks. Every dollar you spend cooling your home, you're spending 25 cents to cool your attic. We do both — and the combined effect is typically a noticeable reduction in dust, allergens, and your APS bill.

Our duct services

Whole-home duct cleaning

Our process uses commercial-grade NIKRO and Rotobrush equipment with HEPA filtration to negative-pressure your duct system, then mechanically agitate and vacuum out every supply, return, and the air handler itself. We document the work with before/after photos so you can actually see the difference.

Aeroseal duct sealing

Aeroseal is a patented sealing technology that pressurizes your duct system and injects an aerosolized polymer that seeks out and seals every leak from the inside. The average @@city home has the equivalent of a 12" hole in their duct system — Aeroseal closes it without ripping out walls.

Duct repair & rerouting

Crushed, disconnected, or improperly sized duct runs cause hot and cold rooms. We diagnose with a manometer and infrared camera, then repair or rebuild as needed for proper airflow.

New duct installation

For new construction, additions, or full duct replacements. We engineer the airflow design from scratch using Manual D — so every room gets the airflow it actually needs, not just whatever the old contractor felt like running.

Signs you need duct cleaning or sealing

  • You see dust on furniture again the day after dusting
  • Allergy or asthma symptoms get worse when the AC kicks on
  • Some rooms are noticeably hotter or colder than others
  • Your APS bill has crept up despite no changes in usage
  • You can see visible dust streaks around supply registers
  • Your home was recently remodeled or had heavy construction
  • You moved into an older home and have never had it cleaned
  • You smell musty or stale air when the system runs

The benefits you'll actually notice

  • Cleaner air — measurable reductions in airborne dust, allergens, and microbial growth
  • Lower energy bills — sealed ducts can cut cooling costs by 15-30%
  • More even temperatures — hot bedroom in the back of the house? Often a duct issue, not an AC issue.
  • Less dust on furniture — the most-cited benefit by our customers
  • Longer AC life — clean coils and proper airflow reduce strain on the compressor
  • Reduced odors — particularly helpful for homes with pets, smokers, or recent water damage
